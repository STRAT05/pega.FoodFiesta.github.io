Lechon

PRICE: ₱5'300.00

Lechon, derived from a Spanish word for roasted suckling pig is one of the most popular dishes in the Philippines.

The slowly-roasted suckling pig is usually stuffed with lemongrass, tamarind, garlic, onions, and chives, and is then roasted on a large bamboo spit over an open fire.
